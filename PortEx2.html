Scenario

Planning Training for Train the Trainer Course

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

In 2025, I designed and developed a scenario-based training experience aimed at empowering trainers to build more effective, learner-centered training plans. This interactive module challenges users to navigate realistic decision-making scenarios, selecting strategies that best align with a trainee’s learning style, motivation level, and existing knowledge base.

Rather than presenting static content, the course simulates the nuanced process of training plan development. Trainers are guided through a series of branching questions, each requiring thoughtful consideration of the trainee’s profile. Their choices are tracked throughout the experience, culminating in feedback that evaluates the strength and suitability of the final training plan.

This project was built to reinforce the principles of adaptive instruction and performance-based planning. It encourages trainers to think critically, personalize their approach, and reflect on how their decisions impact learning outcomes. The result is a dynamic, immersive tool that not only teaches planning — it models it.
