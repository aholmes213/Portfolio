Utilizing Training Plans

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

In 2023, I designed and developed this interactive eLearning course to introduce Airmen to the purpose and practical use of the Career Field Education and Training Plan (CFETP). The course was created to support general knowledge retention and provide learners with a clear understanding of how the CFETP guides career progression and training standards within the Education and Training career field.

This eLearning leverages modern design techniques to enhance interactivity and engagement, including animated page transitions, clickable buttons for deeper exploration, and integrated audio narration to support diverse learning styles. The course was built as a standalone resource but also serves as a foundational tool within broader training programs, helping learners connect policy to practice in a visually appealing and accessible format.
